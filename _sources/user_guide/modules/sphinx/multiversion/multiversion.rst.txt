sphinx-multiversion
===================
A Sphinx extension for building self-hosted versioned documentation.

.. note::

    The initial version of this tool/plugin was taken from `Holzhaus/sphinx-multiversion <https://github.com/Holzhaus/sphinx-multiversion>`_,
    also various patches from this `samuel-emrys/sphinx-multiversion <https://github.com/samuel-emrys/sphinx-multiversion>`_ fork have been applied.
